About The Hotel

Located at 8 Qinian Street in Beijing's Dongcheng District, New World Beijing Hotel blends modern luxury with subtle Chinese elegance. The hotel features 309 sophisticated rooms, a full-service spa, indoor pool, rooftop terrace, and award‑winning dining experiences.

Situated in the historic heart of Beijing, the hotel provides convenient access to the city's most iconic landmarks including the Forbidden City and Temple of Heaven.
